Ways to Connect

CulturalAccessNYC is a growing initiative and we're actively looking for partners, collaborators, and community members who share our commitment to disability equity in NYC's cultural sector.

Cultural Institutions

Looking to improve accessibility at your organization? We offer audits, training, and strategic planning support.

Disabled Artists & Advocates

We center disabled voices in our work. Share your experience, get involved in programming, or join our community network.

Funders & Partners

Interested in supporting this work? We're actively seeking funding partnerships and institutional collaborators.

Press & Media

Covering disability equity in the arts? We're available for comment, interviews, and data on accessibility in NYC's cultural sector.

Our accessibility commitment

We are committed to communicating accessibly. If you need any accommodation to connect with us — including ASL interpretation, captioning, large-print materials, or alternative formats — please let us know and we will do our best to meet your needs.
